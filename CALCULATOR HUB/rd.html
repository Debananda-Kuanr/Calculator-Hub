Use of Calculator

The RD Calculator is a helpful tool to estimate your future savings from a Recurring Deposit. It calculates the maturity amount based on your monthly investment, interest rate, and duration. You can use it to:

  • Compare RD plans with different tenures
  • Estimate interest earned over time
  • Plan your savings goals in advance
  • Choose between Normal and Senior Citizen interest rates

Adjust the values and instantly see how changes affect your total maturity amount.
