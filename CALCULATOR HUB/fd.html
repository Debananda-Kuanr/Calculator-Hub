About the Calculator

The Education Loan EMI Calculator is a tool designed to help users estimate their monthly loan repayment obligations. It takes into account the loan amount, interest rate, and tenure to calculate the Equated Monthly Installment (EMI).

This tool provides a comprehensive breakdown of principal and interest components in each payment, along with a month-wise amortization schedule. It is especially useful for students and parents planning for higher education financing.

By adjusting the inputs, users can visualize how loan terms impact monthly payments and total repayment amount, allowing for informed financial planning.
