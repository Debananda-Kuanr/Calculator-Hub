What is a Gold Loan Calculator?

This tool helps you estimate the value and eligible loan amount based on the weight and caratage of your gold ornaments. It provides a quick idea of how much money you can borrow using your gold as collateral.

Steps to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter the number of ornaments, net weight in grams, and caratage for each.
  2. Click "+ Add Ornaments" to add more entries.
  3. Use the trash icon to remove an entry (at least one row will always remain).
  4. View the gross total and loan eligibility.

Disclaimer

This is a tool for estimation purposes only. Actual loan amounts may vary based on lender policies, current gold rates, and other factors. Please consult with your lender for accurate loan offers.
